### Futhorc

**Words**
* *feng* - grasp, take hold of. 
* *anfeng* - lay hold of, seize. ("on" *feng*.)
    > Anfeng ys yfyl
* *thearle* - severely, sorely (or just "very" in the context of words denoting pain.)
